Our Origin

Vanguard Citizen Services was founded by two friends who share a deep background in both the military and the technology sector. During our time in service, we learned the importance of discipline, technical precision, and looking out for those beside us.

We noticed that as digital tools became a mandatory part of everyday life, many people in the civilian world were being left behind or overwhelmed by the complexity of it all. We decided to bring our expertise home to help our community navigate this new landscape.

FOUNDED BY SERVICE MEMBERS

Why Vanguard?

We chose the name Vanguard because it represents the part of an advancing group that leads the way and scouts the path ahead. In a digital world that changes every single day, we act as the scouts for the Radcliff and Elizabethtown area.

Our mission is to identify threats and build stable foundations so that our neighbors can move forward with total confidence. We believe that technology should be a tool for progress rather than a source of stress.

The Citizen Code

In our organization, we do not have customers or clients. Instead, we have Citizens. This choice of words is a reflection of our core philosophy.

Just as the knights of old held a sacred duty of care toward the people they served, we believe that our technical knowledge comes with a profound responsibility to our community. We are not just fixing computers. We are fulfilling a duty to protect and empower our fellow citizens in the digital age.

The Hardin County Mission

The Radcliff and Elizabethtown corridor is a place where traditional everyday life is now permanently mixed with the digital world. We realize that while technology is everywhere, many of our neighbors are struggling with this transition.

Vanguard exists to bridge that gap. Whether we are securing a small business network or helping a senior navigate their first smartphone, we are committed to ensuring that the people of Hardin County are never left to face the digital frontier alone.
