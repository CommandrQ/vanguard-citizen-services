Terms & Conditions

Warranty Protocols

Tiers 2 through 4 carry a standard 14-day warranty covering workmanship and setup errors. The VIP Tier extends this protection to a 30-day warranty.

Hardware & Service Limitations

We do not perform board-level micro-soldering, liquid damage repair, mobile screen replacements, or enterprise server management. VSR is not liable for data loss; it is the sole responsibility of the client to back up their files prior to deployment.

Installation Boundaries

Smart TV and monitor physical installations are strictly limited to displays measuring up to 50 inches. We do not perform wall mounting of any kind.

On-Site Add-On Services

Additional services requested on-site are limited to one (1) add-on per deployment. These are strictly pending technician time constraints and are not guaranteed to be fulfilled during the initial visit.

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Missing an appointment or failing to provide notification at least two (2) hours prior to the scheduled time will lock the deployment and forfeit the service fee. A new purchase will be required to reset the service. You may reschedule an active deployment up to two (2) times before the service is permanently canceled and your fee is automatically refunded.
