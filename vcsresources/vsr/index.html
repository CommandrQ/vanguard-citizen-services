The Fine Print

The Basics

I’m here to help, but I can’t do everything. If a situation looks unsafe or a job is misrepresented, I reserve the right to change the price or decline the work. Be cool, and I’ll do the same.

Safety First

An adult (18+) has to be there the whole time I’m in your house. Please put the dog in another room and make sure the workspace is clear and safe. I won't move heavy furniture.

What I Don't Do

I don't do tiny screen repairs on phones, liquid damage, or enterprise server work. I'm also not responsible for your data—please back up your stuff before I get there!

Verification (Students)

To get the Student Special ($45), you have to show me a valid ECTC ID or log in to your .edu email when I arrive. If you aren't a student, the price will revert to the standard $55 rate.

Limits

I only set up TVs and monitors up to 50 inches. No wall mounting.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel, let me know at least 2 hours early. If you miss the appointment without telling me, you'll lose the fee and have to purchase again. You get 2 free reschedules before I refund the money and cancel the job.
