    Operational Scope & Bounds

    Physical Jurisdiction

    Physical VSR deployments are currently restricted to the counties immediately surrounding Hardin County and the Louisville Metro Area. Operations requested outside of this active perimeter are subject to travel surcharge rates, or will be strictly limited to Tier 1 Remote Support.

    Hardware & Service Limitations

    To ensure efficiency and quality, VSR explicitly does not perform the following services:

    • Board-level micro-soldering.
    • Liquid damage remediation.
    • Mobile device screen or battery replacements.
    • Enterprise-grade server or database administration.

    Data Liability Protocol

    Digital sovereignty requires operational responsibility. While our technicians exercise extreme caution, VSR is not liable for incidental data loss. Clients are solely responsible for backing up critical files prior to any physical or remote deployment.

    Pre-Deployment Checklist

    To maximize your operational time, please ensure the following prior to technician arrival:

    • Have all necessary administrative passwords written down.
    • Verify all power and connection cables are easily accessible.
    • Clear the physical operating space around the hardware.
