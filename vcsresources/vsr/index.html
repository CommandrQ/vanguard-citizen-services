The Fine Print

Operational Rights

Vanguard Support & Repair is committed to quality help, but the team reserves the right to decline or modify services if a situation is deemed unsafe or if the job scope was misrepresented during dispatch.

Safety & Attendance

An adult (18+) must be present for the entire duration of any on-site deployment. Pets must be secured, and the workspace must be clear and safe. Our specialists do not move heavy furniture.

Service Limits

We do not perform board-level micro-soldering, mobile screen repairs, or enterprise server management. Vanguard Support & Repair is not responsible for data loss; please back up all files prior to arrival.

Student Verification

The Student Special ($45) is for active ECTC/KCTCS students only. Valid student ID or .edu login verification is required on-site. If verification is not provided, the price reverts to the standard $55 rate.

Install Limits

Monitor and TV setups are limited to displays up to 50 inches. Wall mounting is not provided.

Cancellations

Please provide at least 2 hours' notice for cancellations. Missing an appointment without notice results in forfeiture of the fee. Two reschedules are permitted before a service is canceled.
